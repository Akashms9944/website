FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How long does a typical project take? +
Most brand identity projects take 4–6 weeks. Full website builds range from 6–12 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a precise timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you work with startups? +
Absolutely. We love working with ambitious startups and often offer flexible payment structures for early-stage companies with big visions.
What information do you need to get started? +
A brief overview of your business, target audience, goals, and budget is enough to kick off a discovery call. We handle the rest from there.
Can you work with our existing brand? +
Yes — we offer brand evolution services where we refine and modernise existing identities rather than starting from scratch.
Do you offer ongoing support? +
Yes. All projects include a support period, and we offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing design, development, and marketing needs.
Who owns the final deliverables? +
You do. Upon final payment, all intellectual property, source files, and assets transfer entirely to you.
How do revisions work? +
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. We present our work in structured sessions and gather consolidated feedback to keep projects on track.
Are you remote-only? +
We're remote-first with team members across multiple time zones, but we can arrange in-person workshops for local clients and enterprise projects.
